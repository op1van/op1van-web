THAT’S WHY MY MUSIC STARTS WITH RECORDED BITS OF SONGS FROM VARIOUS CITIES AND COUNTRIES, WHICH I LATER REASSEMBLE LIKE A PUZZLE ON THE OP-1. THE TRACKS TITLES WILL HINT AT THEIR ORIGINS. SOME PARTS WERE LATER REWORKED IN THE STUDIO, AND PARTIALLY VOCALS WERE ADDED. IT IS A UNIQUE EXPERIENCE OF RECORDING A MOMENT, AN OCCURRENCE THAT CANNOT BE PREDICTED. BECAUSE YOU HAD TO PRESS THE "RECORD" BUTTON IN ADVANCE: EVEN BEFORE THE DESIRED PIECE OF MUSIC STARTED PLAYING. THAT’S THE CONCEPT. AND THEN IT’S A MATTER OF TASTE.
